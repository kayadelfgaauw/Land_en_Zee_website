Hotel Studio on the Water

Book Now

The studios on the water are located on the park of the Resort, on the ground floor.

  • Private terrace with garden furniture
  • Kitchen with stove, kettle, and cleaning kit (no kitchen linen)
  • Air conditioning
  • Safe
  • Refrigerator
  • Small seating area in the bedroom
  • The studios have an area of 28m²
  • Bathroom with shower, toilet and towels
  • Hairdryer
  • 2 box spring beds with a double top mattress (including bed linen)
  • Possibilities for placing an extra bed
  • Small table with two chairs
  • Flat screen TV
  • Coffee and tea facilities
  • View of the water
  • Pets are allowed in some hotel studios (on request)
