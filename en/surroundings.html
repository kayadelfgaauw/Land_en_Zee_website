Explore the surroundings

The ideal base

Resort Land & Zee is an ideal base for discovering the versatile Schouwen-Duiveland. From the resort you can cycle or walk along dunes, dykes and Zeeland villages.

Hop on your bike and explore the routes along the dunes. Visit nature reserve Plan Tureluur for extraordinary birds, discover the impressive Delta Works, learn about the history of Zeeland at the Flood Museum. For action and fun, the Waterjump at the Brouwersdam is highly recommended. Discover and taste local wines at De Kleine Schorre. The historic town of Zierikzee is also just a short distance away.

Whether you love nature, culture or culinary delights: in the vicinity of Resort Land & Zee there is plenty to experience all year round.

At 150 metres distance

Beach

The beach is only about 150 metres from Resort Land & Zee!

The dune crossings at Our Seaside and Beachclub Perry's are also accessible for wheelchairs and buggies.

For the dogs

There are some general rules when walking with your dog on the Schouwen-Duiveland beaches. The dog must be on a lead in built-up areas, but outside those areas it is as free as a bird (unless otherwise indicated).

On the beach and in the sea, dogs are allowed off-lead from 1st October to 30th April (unless otherwise stated). From 1st May to 1st October, this is allowed from 19:00-10:00, outside those times only on a lead.

Note: On the Verklikkerstrand, dogs must be on a lead all day and night from 1st April to 15th September, due to nesting birds.
