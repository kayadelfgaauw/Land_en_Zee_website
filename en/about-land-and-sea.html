About Resort Land & Zee

At Resort Land & Zee, it is all about hospitality, tranquillity and nature. What began 18 years ago as a family business has grown into a place where guests feel truly welcome. That warm, personal atmosphere has always remained, and you will notice it in everything we do.

You will be staying in the heart of one of the most beautiful natural areas in the Netherlands. The North Sea beach and the dunes lie just behind your hotel room, followed by the forest and the vast 'Domeinen'. There are no crowds, no industry or high-rise buildings in sight; only sea, sky, beach and space to truly unwind.

Whether you come to relax, explore or enjoy the Zeeland nature: at Resort Land & Zee, you will find the space, peace and personal attention that make your stay special.
