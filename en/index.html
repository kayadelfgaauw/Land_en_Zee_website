WELCOME TO
RESORT LAND & ZEE

On Schouwen-Duiveland, hidden behind the dunes of the North Sea beach and the Grevelingenmeer, lies at the mouth of the Brouwersdam, Resort Land & Zee.

In this unique water environment you can spot a seal, see a deer and enjoy the beautiful sunsets. With the beach about 150 meters away, you sleep at night to the rhythm of the sound of the sea.
