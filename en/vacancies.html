Vacancy Chef

Do you have a passion for cooking and do you like to work in a committed team? Then this is your chance. Restaurant Zeeuws Verlangen, located at Resort Land & Zee, is looking for a Full-time chef or kitchen assistant with ambition. Multiple options are possible, such as Part-time or Freelance (ZZP).

What will you do?

As a chef or kitchen assistant, you help daily with preparing and arranging lunch and dinner dishes. You work with fresh ingredients, check orders, and monitor quality. You also think along about menus and presentation. You follow the HACCP guidelines and contribute to a professional kitchen environment.

You are independent, creative, accurate, and stress-resistant. You like working in a team, but you also know your way around independently. Experience is a plus, but we also invite motivated candidates with a willingness to learn to respond.

What do we offer?

  • Contract with a view to a permanent contract
  • Good salary and excellent secondary employment conditions
  • Travel allowance, even if you live nearby
  • Collegiate working atmosphere with room for your own ideas
  • Development opportunities within this growing company

Interested?
