Ecolodge

Book Now

The Ecolodge is a modern, sustainably built lodge with a magnificent view over the water. This stylish accommodation is designed with an eye for both comfort and the environment, offering a wonderful place to fully unwind.

The lodge features two bedrooms:

  • One bedroom with two box spring beds and a double top mattress.
  • One bedroom with two single box spring beds, each with its own top mattress.

You will also find a cosy seating area with a sofa and armchair, a dining area and a fully equipped living space with coffee and tea making facilities and a flat screen television. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, washbasin, toilet and hairdryer.

Outside you can enjoy the view over the water and the tranquillity of the resort on the private terrace with table, chairs and sun loungers.

On arrival, the beds will be made up and towels will be ready for you.

Pets are not allowed in the Ecolodge.
