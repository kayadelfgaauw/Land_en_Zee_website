Holiday Home 6-persons

Book Now

This family stay is located in the old farmhouse of Resort Land & Zee, just 100 metres from the beach. Ideal for families and groups who want to enjoy peace, nature and the sea.

The home features three comfortable bedrooms:

  • One bedroom with two box spring beds with a double top mattress.
  • One bedroom with a double box spring bed with double top mattress.
  • One bedroom with two single box spring beds, each with its own top mattress.

There is also a cosy living room with an open kitchen, a flat screen television and a bathroom with shower. Outside you can enjoy the fresh sea air on your private terrace with table and chairs.

On arrival, the beds will be made up and towels will be ready for you, so you can relax immediately.

Pets are not allowed in this accommodation.
